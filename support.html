‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import my trip screenshots?

Use the Import tab in the app to select photos from your camera roll. The app will automatically detect and process trip screenshots from Uber, Lyft, and other ride-sharing services.

How do I access premium features?

Tap on any premium feature to start a 5-day free trial. After the trial, you can subscribe to continue accessing premium features like Analytics, History, and Auto Import.

Can I export my trip data?

Yes! Premium users can export their trip data to CSV, Excel, or PDF formats. Go to the History tab and use the export feature.

How do I set earning goals?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Goals to set daily, weekly, and monthly earning targets. The app will track your progress and notify you when you reach your goals.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Your trip data, including locations and earnings, is stored locally on your device and is never shared with third parties. Only subscription information is shared with our payment processor.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your iPhone's Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions, or contact us for assistance.

üîß Troubleshooting

The app isn't detecting my screenshots

Make sure you've granted photo library access to Revealed+. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Photos to check permissions.

My statistics aren't updating

Try refreshing the app by pulling down on the Today or Analytics screen. If issues persist, try closing and reopening the app.

I'm having trouble with auto-import

Auto-import requires background app refresh to be enabled. Go to Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Background App Refresh and ensure it's turned on for Revealed+.
