Privacy Policy

Learn how Revealed+ collects, uses, and protects your personal information

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our Revealed+ mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following types of information:

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Trip Data: Information about your trips, including locations, distances, durations, earnings, and trip screenshots. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • User Settings: Your app preferences, earning goals, and configuration settings are stored locally.
  • Work Session Data: Information about your work sessions, break times, and real-time earnings calculations.
  • Analytics Data: Usage patterns and app interaction data for improving app functionality.

Information Transmitted to Third-Party Services

  • Subscription Data: When you subscribe to premium features, we use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat receives your device identifier, subscription status, and feature access information.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, Apple processes the transaction and provides us with basic purchase confirmation data.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (device model, operating system) may be shared with RevenueCat for subscription management.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens for push notifications are shared with Apple's notification services.

Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your trip data, including locations and earnings, remains stored locally on your device and is not shared with any third-party services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App and services
  • Process and manage your in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Manage subscription access and feature entitlements

Sharing Your Information

We share your information with the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and feature access. RevenueCat receives your device identifier, subscription status, and feature usage data. Their privacy policy can be found at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Apple: Apple processes in-app purchases and provides push notification services. Apple's privacy policy applies to their services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using Core Data. Some data may be backed up to iCloud if you have enabled iCloud backup for our App. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

Third-party services (RevenueCat, Apple) store data according to their respective privacy policies and security practices.

Data Retention

Local data stored on your device is retained until you delete the app or manually remove the data. Subscription and purchase data is retained by Apple and RevenueCat according to their respective retention policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your personal information
  • Objecting to our processing of your information
  • Requesting restriction of processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Third-party services (RevenueCat, Apple) may store data in various locations worldwide. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
