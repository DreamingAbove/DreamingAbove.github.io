How It Works

Simply input your original message, select the sender's MBTI type (your perspective) and the recipient's MBTI type (their perspective). TypeTalk's advanced algorithm, informed by comprehensive MBTI profiles, will then instantly rephrase your message to align with the target personality's communication style, relational needs, and preferences.

Whether it's a critical email, a sensitive personal conversation, or a professional interaction, TypeTalk helps you tailor your words for maximum impact and understanding.
