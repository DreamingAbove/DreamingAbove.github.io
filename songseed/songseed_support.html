Common questions

What is SongSeed?

SongSeed helps songwriters and producers analyze songs to surface creative DNA, then grow new ideas—from lyrics to production-style prompts—via Song Analysis or the Full Inspiration Workflow.

How does Apple Music work in the app?

Song search uses the Apple Music catalog on your device. SongSeed does not stream full tracks on this website; music discovery and analysis happen in the iOS app, with Apple Music as applicable.

I’m having trouble with purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are managed by Apple. Check your Apple ID subscription settings, and include your purchase receipt or transaction details when you email support so we can help faster.
