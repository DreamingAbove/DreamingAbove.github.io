Let's Hang

"I will be here for a bit, come hang if you want."

Let's Hang is a spontaneous meetup app where you can create short-lived, location-based events called Hangs. Share where you are with friends and invite them to join you in real life.

How It Works

Let's Hang makes spontaneous meetups effortless. Create a Hang at your current location with a short visibility window (default 30 minutes), and your friends can discover it on a map or list view. They can express intent with simple actions like "On my way" or "I am here" - no heavy planning required.

The focus is on low pressure, in the moment hangs, not long term event planning. Just announce where you'll be for a short time and let your trusted circle know.

Key Features

Create Hangs

Start a Hang at your current location with just one tap. Set a short visibility window and optional description.

Discover Friends' Hangs

See active Hangs from your friends on both a map view and list view, sorted by time remaining.

Map View

Visualize active Hangs with neon-styled pins on an interactive map centered on your location.

Presence States

Express your intent with minimal actions: "On my way" or "I am here" - visible to the host and other attendees.

Friend Management

Add and manage friends with a simple bidirectional friend request system.

Privacy Controls

Choose visibility: share with all friends or create private Hangs with specific invitees.

Safety Features

Built-in blocking, reporting, and the ability to end a Hang early for your peace of mind.

80s Vaporwave Aesthetic

Enjoy a nostalgic, fun visual style with neon gradients and retro-inspired details while keeping the UI minimal and fast.

Why Let's Hang?

  • Spontaneous, Not Scheduled: No heavy planning or calendar coordination. Just announce where you are and let friends drop in.
  • Low Pressure: Hangs are short-lived (30 minutes default) and casual - perfect for spontaneous meetups.
  • Location-Based: See where your friends are hanging right now, not where they plan to be next week.
  • Trusted Circle: Only friends can see your Hangs - no public feeds or strangers.
  • Minimal Actions: One tap to start a Hang, simple buttons to express intent - no complex RSVP systems.
  • Real World Presence: Focus on actual in-person hangs, not endless scrolling or virtual interactions.
