Common questions

What is CatalystIQ?

CatalystIQ is an AI-powered iOS app that helps active traders judge whether recent news and SEC filings about a stock are likely to attract short-term trading attention. Enter a ticker, run an analysis, and get a CatalystIQ Score (0–100) with a plain-English breakdown.

What is the CatalystIQ Score?

The CatalystIQ Score is a 0–100 gauge of how likely today’s catalysts are to draw trader attention. It is paired with sentiment, catalyst type, insight, and a checklist of why traders may care. It is an analytical signal—not a buy or sell recommendation.

What is the difference between the free tier and CatalystIQ Plus?

Free tier: You bring your own API keys for OpenAI, Finnhub, and Marketaux. Requests go directly from your device to those providers.

CatalystIQ Plus: A subscription ($5.99/month or $29.99/year) that includes managed API access through our servers—no key setup—and up to 100 analyses per day, plus additional market context features.

Which API keys do I need for the free tier?

You will need API keys from OpenAI, Finnhub, and Marketaux. Enter them in CatalystIQ Settings. You are responsible for your own accounts, billing, and usage limits with those providers. SEC filing and Nasdaq halt data use public endpoints and do not require keys from those services.

How do trade halt notifications work?

CatalystIQ can monitor Nasdaq’s public trade-halt feed and send local notifications on your device when halts occur. Enable alerts in Settings and choose whether to notify for tickers you have analyzed or for all halted stocks. Notifications are scheduled on-device; CatalystIQ does not use a remote push server for halts.

How do I cancel my CatalystIQ Plus subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone, select CatalystIQ Plus, and cancel. You can also use the subscription management / Customer Center options in the App where available. Cancellation stops future renewals; access continues until the end of the current billing period unless otherwise stated by Apple.

Is CatalystIQ investment advice?

No. CatalystIQ is an analytical tool only. Scores and AI-generated insights may be wrong or incomplete. You are solely responsible for your trading decisions. See our Terms of Service for full disclaimers.
