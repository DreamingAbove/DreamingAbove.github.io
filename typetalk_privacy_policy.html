Privacy Policy

Learn how TypeTalk collects, uses, and protects your personal information

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our TypeTalk mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following types of information:

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • App Settings: Your preferences including selected MBTI types, translation scenarios, tone preferences, and app customization settings
  • Translation History: Your saved translations and conversation history (stored locally on your device only)
  • Premium Status: Information about your subscription or purchase status

User Content We Process

To provide our core MBTI translation functionality, we process:

  • Text Messages: The messages you enter for translation
  • MBTI Type Selections: Your selected source and target MBTI types
  • Context Information: Selected scenario context and tone preferences

This data is transmitted to OpenAI's API for processing and is not stored on our servers. OpenAI processes this data in accordance with their privacy policy.

Usage Data We Collect

We collect analytics data to improve our App, including:

  • App Usage: App opens, session duration, screen views, and feature usage patterns
  • Translation Metrics: Number of translations performed and usage milestones
  • Performance Data: App crashes, errors, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, and language settings

This data is collected through Firebase Analytics and is not linked to your personal identity.

Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases, we collect:

  • Transaction Data: Purchase records, subscription status, and transaction identifiers
  • Purchase History: Information about your premium subscription or one-time purchases

This information is processed through RevenueCat and Apple's App Store to manage your premium features and entitlements.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Functionality: To translate your messages between different MBTI communication styles using AI processing
  • Manage Purchases: To process in-app purchases and manage your premium subscription status
  • Improve the App: To analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and enhance app performance and features
  • Send Notifications: To send you relevant notifications about milestones, tips, and app updates (with your permission)
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App and improve user experience

Third-Party Services

OpenAI

Purpose: Core MBTI translation functionality

Data Shared: Text messages you submit for translation, MBTI type selections, and context preferences

Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

Purpose: Analytics, performance monitoring, remote configuration, push notifications, and in-app messaging

Data Shared: Payment information and transaction details

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We only share data with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide our App's functionality and services.

Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: Your app settings, preferences, and translation history are stored locally on your device using secure iOS data storage methods
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted to third-party services (OpenAI, Firebase, RevenueCat) is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your translation content on our servers. Messages are processed in real-time and not retained
  • Third-Party Security: Our third-party service providers maintain their own security measures and are responsible for protecting data they process

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain different types of data for varying periods:

  • Local Data: Settings and translation history stored on your device are retained until you delete the app or manually remove the data
  • Translation Content: Messages sent to OpenAI for translation are not stored by us and are subject to OpenAI's data retention policies
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics data is retained according to Firebase's default retention periods (typically 2-14 months)
  • Purchase Data: Transaction records are retained as required by Apple and RevenueCat for legal and accounting purposes

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access and Correction: You can access and modify your data directly through the app's settings
  • Deletion: You can delete your local data by removing the app or clearing data within the app
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can disable analytics tracking through iOS Settings > Privacy > Analytics & Improvements
  • Notification Control: You can manage push notification permissions through iOS Settings
  • Data Portability: You can export your translation history from within the app
  • Objection: You can object to processing by discontinuing use of the app or specific features

To exercise these rights or request data deletion from third-party services, please contact us using the information provided below. Note that certain data processed by third parties (OpenAI, Firebase, RevenueCat) is subject to their respective privacy policies and data retention practices.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

International Data Transfers

When you use our App, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence:

  • OpenAI: Data is processed on servers located in the United States and other countries where OpenAI operates
  • Firebase: Data may be processed on Google servers worldwide
  • RevenueCat: Purchase data is processed in the United States

These countries may have different data protection laws than your country. By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place through our third-party service providers' compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
